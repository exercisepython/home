About

ExercisePython is a website that offers beginner Python exercises

Welcome to ExercisePython! This website was build for everybody who is currently learning Python, and/or wants to
apply his knowledge in fun and challenging exercises. Additionally, the Discussions section offers a quick
explanation to refresh the knowledge about the given topic. Of course, at the end of each exercise an answer is provided too.
To stay updated about new exercises or blogposts, make sure to follow us on Twitter.
